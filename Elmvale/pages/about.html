About Us

Our history, mission, and vision for a greener community

Our History

Founded in the early 1900s, the Elmvale Horticultural Society has served the community by fostering a love for gardening and environmental stewardship. Through the years, we've helped beautify public spaces, organized numerous educational events, and contributed to local environmental conservation efforts.

Our Mission

We are a non-profit organization dedicated to promoting the art and science of horticulture. Our mission is to inspire, educate, and empower our community through gardening and environmental education. We aim to create a greener, healthier environment through planting, community involvement, and the promotion of sustainable gardening practices.

Our Vision

Our vision is to cultivate a community that is passionate about gardening and environmental conservation, where people are actively engaged in making their homes and public spaces more beautiful and sustainable. We envision a thriving green space in every corner of Elmvale, where people come together to learn and grow.

Our Values

  • Respect for nature and the environment
  • Commitment to sustainability and eco-friendly practices
  • Community engagement and collaboration
  • Support for local gardens and public beautification projects

Bylaws & Code of Conduct

  • We respect all members and the community.
  • We promote sustainable and eco-friendly gardening practices.
  • Members must adhere to the society's rules during events and meetings.
  • Complete bylaws are available upon request or at meetings.
